Quentin Glorieux has received an ERC Proof of Concept 2026 grant for **MIstiQU-MED** — *AtoMIc-based Quantum magnetometry meets Ultrasound for Medical Early Detection*. Hosted by Sorbonne Université, the project will explore the innovation potential of quantum magnetometry combined with ultrasound for early medical detection.

ERC Proof of Concept grants support the first steps in translating ERC research results towards commercial or societal applications. MIstiQU-MED is among the six projects recognised in the first 2026 wave at [CNRS Physique](https://www.inp.cnrs.fr/fr/cnrsinfo/erc-proof-concept-2026-6-projets-laureats-cnrs-physique). Read the [CNRS announcement](https://www.cnrs.fr/fr/actualite/erc-proof-concept-2026-laureats-du-cnrs-pour-la-premiere-vague) and the [ERC recipient list](https://erc.europa.eu/system/files/2026-07/erc-2026-poc-results_2.pdf).
