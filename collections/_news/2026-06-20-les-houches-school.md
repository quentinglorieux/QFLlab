A nice school is going on this 2 weeks at Les Houches.
The school explore current and emerging topics in quantum optics, ultra-cold atoms & molecules, quantum simulation, and photonics.

Jeanne Duboeuf and Ethan Uzan are attending the school, and will present their work during the poster session.

And Quentin is organizing wiht Lauriane Chomaz and Igor Ferrier-Barbut.
