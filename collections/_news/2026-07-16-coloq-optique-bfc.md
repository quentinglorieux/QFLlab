Quentin Glorieux is an invited speaker in the COLOQ session on **Fluides de lumière et solitons** at [OPTIQUE 2026](https://www.sfoptique.org/pages/sfo/congres-optique/optique-bfc-2026/programme.html), organised by the Société Française d'Optique.

The session, chaired by Marc Cheneau, brings together Quentin, Pierre Suret and other colleagues to discuss fluids of light and solitons within the **Lasers et Optique Quantique** programme.
